Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, recognise you when you return, and provide a better user experience. Cookies may be set by the website itself or by third-party services (such as analytics providers or font providers).

Cookies we use

The honest answer: we don't currently use any cookies ourselves.

Alder CRM's website is a static HTML site without authentication, analytics, or tracking systems. We do not set cookies, use cookies to track behaviour, or store data about you in cookies.

Third-party connections

Although we don't set cookies, we do load fonts from Google Fonts. When your browser fetches these fonts, it makes a request to Google's servers. Google's privacy policy covers their use of any data collected in that request.

If you prefer not to load external fonts, you can block requests to fonts.googleapis.com in your browser settings, though this may affect the website's appearance.

Analytics

We do not currently use any analytics or tracking software.

Future changes: We may add privacy-focused analytics (such as Plausible) in the future to understand how visitors use our site. If we do, we will update this policy and clearly explain what data is collected. Any analytics tool we choose will be selected for its commitment to privacy and compliance with UK GDPR and UK PECR.

Managing cookies in your browser

Most browsers allow you to control how cookies are handled. You can typically:

  • View cookies stored on your device
  • Delete all cookies or cookies from a specific site
  • Block cookies from specific domains
  • Enable "Do Not Track" settings

For guidance, search for cookie settings in your browser's help documentation. Note that blocking all cookies may affect functionality on some websites.

Changes to this policy

We may update this cookie policy from time to time to reflect changes in our technology, legal requirements, or practices. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
